Mayonaka Dance OFF

Raytrax Collaborative Album

RELEASE DATE: October 24th, 2022


ARTIST NOTES

I had previously contributed songs to other collaboration albums and was among a small group of artists that got together to make a sequel to the compilation album Mayonaka Dancehall which had become popular on Beat Saber. This was the first song I had written that included multiple genres such as Electro Swing and Hardcore EDM and challenged me to work outside my comfort zone. I was influenced by one of the primary contributors (ReeK) who is known for his use of multiple genres. As usual the song was produced using REAPER and is my longest and most technical song to date. It allowed me to work with artists from around the world demonstrating the worldwide nature of the rhythm game music community.

Boundless Adventure

2ND SINGLE

RELEASE DATE: October 24th, 2022


ARTIST NOTES

I was asked to write this composition to accompany the trailer for a new Minecraft server from the founders of GaiaMC and Planetarycraft. Normally I compose electronic music but the nature of this game needed a more orchestral setting which I felt was a challenge for me to pursue. I was heavily influenced by the songs Fly Far Bounce by 猫又Master and Destr0yer by 削除, both of which artists I admire. The final version was produced using REAPER DAW and was attached to trailer which was posted to YouTube. This song was my first collaboration with a game production team and I gained valuable experience working with the developers.

iSOCENTRIC MOMENTUM

DACHiTRAX COLLABORATIVE ALBUM

RELEASE DATE: September 5th, 2021


ARTIST NOTES

After the release of the collaboration album THX for XX Years, the other contributors to the album and I decided that we wanted to collaborate on a second album. At the time it was the longest and most technical song I had written and challenged me to write creatively without repetition in a genre with which I previously had little experience. My influences for this track were primarily the artists PSYQUI and lapix and the song was produced using REAPER and released to the DACHiTRAX music circle.

ARTIST NOTES

I had downloaded it to compete in the Arcaea Song Contest which was running at the time, but I struggled to learn how to use the software and couldn't submit anything.
